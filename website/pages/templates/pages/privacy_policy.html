Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6/26/2023

Thank you for using our web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a secure and seamless experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle the information generated and processed by our web app. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

1. Information Generated and Processed

Our web app scrapes data from various Indian news channels' YouTube channels and processes it using advanced algorithms to generate insightful charts and analytics. This data includes top trending news topics over the week, the most talked-about famous personalities, and news topics covered by different channels. Please note that we do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers from our users.

2. Use of Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and optimize our web application's functionality. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain anonymous information such as a unique identifier. These cookies help us analyze website traffic, customize your preferences, and improve our services. By using our web application, you consent to the use of cookies. You can choose to disable or delete cookies in your browser settings; however, please note that doing so may affect the functionality and performance of our web application.

3. Data Usage and Security

The data generated and processed by our web app is used solely for the purpose of providing you with the charts and analytics insights. We do not sell, share, or disclose this data to any third parties. We prioritize data security and implement industry-standard measures to protect the information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. User Data Collection

Our web app does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers from our users. You can freely browse and access the charts and analytics without providing any personal information.

5. Downloaded Charts

You have the option to download the generated charts and analytics from our web app. Please note that once the data is downloaded, it is solely under your responsibility, and we do not have control over its usage or security. We recommend taking necessary precautions to protect the downloaded data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date at the beginning of this policy. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
